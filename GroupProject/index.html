Our Mission

Ever find yourself staring at a pile of clothes in the morning with absolutely no idea what to wear? It feels like there are so many options, and yet none at all. myWardrobe is meant to help you inspire yourself with items from your very own closet. Style is always changing, but you don't have to be a fashionista or keep up with all the latest trends to be stylish. You just have to be creative.

Being creative is easier said than done - most of the time. myWardrobe is a personalized wardrobe assistant created to help you create outfits with your own clothes that you would never have thought to try before. Style is always changing, so let us be the catalyst. "I'm for style. Fashion changes too quickly." - Coco Chanel "What you wear is how you present yourself to the world, especially today, when human contacts are so quick. Fashion is instant language." - Muiccia Prada
